Forgery is a contra dance duo featuring Ben Jackson on fiddle and Will Wheeler on guitar and feet. The two first met at the barnless barn dance at Santa Clara's Forge Garden, and have been playing contras all over the San Francisco Bay Area, and recently at the Northwest Folklife Festival.
